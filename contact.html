Common questions

Before you tee off.

FAQ · 01Do you accept course review requests?

Yes — if you manage or represent a course within roughly 60 miles of Pittsburgh, reach out. Every review reflects honest personal experience, and we'll always tell you if it's sponsored or comped.

FAQ · 02Are sponsored reviews disclosed?

Always, in the lede. Any post involving a sponsored visit, free round, or compensation is labeled clearly. Editorial opinions remain our own — bad sponsored rounds still get bad scores.

FAQ · 03How do I correct something in a review?

Pick hole 03 above and tell us which line. We update the post, add a footnote with the date of the correction, and credit you by name unless you'd rather we didn't.

FAQ · 04Can I write a guest review?

We're open to it once we've played the course ourselves — that's the threshold. If you've got a course we haven't touched, send us the tip first and we'll line up a round.

FAQ · 05Why didn't you reply yet?

Most likely we're on a course. Second most likely it slipped past 48 hours — please send a polite nudge. Third: spam filter ate it. If a week has passed, try @pghgolfs on Instagram.
