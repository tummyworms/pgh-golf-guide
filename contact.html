Common Questions

Do you accept course review requests?

Yes — if you manage or represent a course within ~60 miles of Pittsburgh, reach out. All reviews reflect honest personal experience.

Are sponsored reviews disclosed?

Always. Any post involving a sponsored visit or compensation is clearly labeled. Editorial opinions remain our own.

Can I suggest a course to review?

Absolutely — use the form and select "Course Suggestion." We work through the list over the season.
